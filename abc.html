Our Business Model

1

Businesses Pay for Waste Solutions

Hotels, caterers and event venues pay us to handle their surplus food responsibly through subscription plans or per-pickup fees.

2

We Redistribute Edible Food

Our logistics network delivers quality food to orphanages, shelters and communities in need within hours.

3

Non-Edible Waste Generates Revenue

What can't be eaten gets converted to compost or biogas, creating additional income streams.
